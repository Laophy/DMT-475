Discover Wisconsin's Trails

Nature Connects

We believe spending time outdoors is transformational. Nature boosts our physical and mental health, engenders respect for ourselves, and invites deeper connections with the environment. Hike Wisconsin showcases the wide variety of landscapes the state has to offer, from bluffs and waterfalls, to dense forests and open prairies. There are trails for all ages and all skill levels.

Featured Trails

Doughboys Nature Trail

This easy loop trail offers stunning views of Copper Falls, Brownstone Falls and the Tyler Forks cascades.

Chippewa Moraine Circle Trail

This 5 mile scenic loop winds through several kettle lakes left behind by the glaciers. Trail is moderate with some hills.

Big Manitou Falls to Little Manitou Falls

Spring or fall, this easy out-and-back trail is sure to delight. Visit two waterfalls and enjoy great views of the Black River.
