Wisconsin Has So Much to Offer

There are over 2,500 miles of hiking trails weaving through Wisconsin's state parks alone! From the Driftless region in southwestern Wisconsin to Lake Superior in the north and Lake Michigan to the east, and everything in between, our state has so much variety. And hiking in Wisconsin is one of the most rewarding ways to explore our lush national and state parks and forests. Hiking is also super easy to do. Simply put one foot in front of the other and repeat. So what are you waiting for? Choose a destination, lace up your shoes, and head out.
